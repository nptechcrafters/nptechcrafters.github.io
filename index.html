About us

NP TechCrafters is an MSME-approved EdTech startup dedicated to empowering the youth and shaping the future of learning and technology. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements by providing students with real-time, hands-on project experience. We strongly believe that skill development is the foundation of a successful career. That is why we offer virtual internships across diverse domains, including Website Development, Data Science, Mobile Application Development, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning. Each internship is carefully designed to give students practical exposure, industry-level tasks, personalized mentorship, and real-world problem-solving experience. Along with internships, NP TechCrafters also offers industry-relevant certification courses in essential technologies such as C, Java, Python, Website Designing, and Full-Stack Development. Our goal is to make every learner job-ready, confident, and capable of building real solutions. At NP TechCrafters, we are committed to supporting students not only with skills but also with career guidance, project-based learning, and continuous mentorship, helping them unlock new opportunities and strengthen their professional journey.

Our Services

At NP TechCrafters, we specialize in developing personalized, high-quality software solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients. We believe that every business has a different vision, and our job is to transform that vision into a digital reality.

Our development process is built on close collaboration with clients, ensuring that every feature, design decision, and functionality aligns perfectly with the business goals. From the initial idea to the final deployment, we maintain transparent communication to ensure our work remains focused on the desired outcome.

We offer a wide range of services including:

  • Custom Software Development for businesses of all sizes
  • Mobile Application Development (Android & iOS)
  • Website Designing and Full-Stack Development
  • AI & Machine Learning Solutions
  • Data Analytics and Automation Tools
  • Cloud-based Systems and API Integrations
  • Enterprise-Level Digital Solutions

Our team is committed to delivering robust, scalable, and future-ready software that helps businesses grow, become efficient, and stay competitive in the digital world.

NP TechCrafters, as an MSME-approved EdTech and IT development company, continues to serve both students and enterprises, building innovative solutions while nurturing the next generation of tech professionals.
